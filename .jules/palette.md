## 2024-05-18 - Missing ARIA Labels and Focus Rings on Icon-only Buttons
**Learning:** Custom icon-only buttons (like modal close buttons "×" and tab close buttons "×" and new tab buttons "+") lack ARIA labels and `:focus-visible` styling, hindering keyboard and screen reader accessibility. Since `button:focus` and `:focus-visible` are overridden with `outline: none` in some places or customized for standard buttons, we need to explicitly provide `:focus-visible` states for our custom `.modal-close`, `.tab-close` and `.tab-new` button classes so keyboard navigation works as expected.
**Action:** Added `aria-label` to all icon-only buttons (`.modal-close`, `.tab-close`, `.tab-new`) across all modal and tab components. Included targeted `:focus-visible` CSS rules (`outline: 2px solid var(--pastel-purple); outline-offset: 2px;`) to these elements to reinstate visible focus rings without affecting mouse users.
