## 2024-05-18 - TabBar Component Accessibility Enhancements
**Learning:** In the `TabBar` component, the interactive tabs lacked keyboard navigability (no `tabIndex`, no `onKeyDown` handling) and semantic ARIA roles. Furthermore, when adding `onKeyDown` to custom interactive tab components that also contain internal interactive elements (like the `.tab-close` button), it's crucial to check `e.target === e.currentTarget` to ensure events bubbling up from the child close button don't inadvertently trigger the parent tab's `onKeyDown` action.
**Action:** Add `role="tablist"` to the container, `role="tab"`, `aria-selected`, and `tabIndex={0}` to the individual tabs, along with an `onKeyDown` handler that specifically checks `e.target === e.currentTarget` before proceeding. Add descriptive `aria-label`s to the icon-only `.tab-close` and `.tab-new` buttons.
