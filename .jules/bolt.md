## 2024-03-15 - [React Rendering Optimization]
**Learning:** `Sidebar.jsx` filters and groups events based on `folders` and `events` data arrays during every single render. This leads to redundant array traversals and nested loop executions (`folders.find()` inside `forEach`) whenever component state changes (such as expanding folders, hovering, or searching), acting as a performance bottleneck when dealing with larger datasets.
**Action:** Use `useMemo` hooks to cache the results of filtered lists (`filteredEvents`, `rootFolders`) and heavy grouping computations (`eventsByFolder`, `rootEvents`). Ensure the correct dependency arrays are specified, especially when referencing `getChildFolders` and making sure `toLowerCase()` search term conversion is pulled out of the internal filter loop if possible. Always inspect components with large datasets for similar redundant work on re-renders.
